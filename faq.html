Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any questions, you're in the right place! Below are some of the most common questions we get.

What is Speak Up Scholars?
Speak Up Scholars is a non-profit organization that aims to teach children public speaking and debate skills while also offering tutoring in subjects such as English, Spanish, and Math.
How can I become a tutor?
You can sign up to become a tutor by filling out our online tutor application form, available on the Tutor Sign-Up page. We’re always looking for passionate individuals to join our team!
What subjects do you offer tutoring in?
We offer tutoring in English, Spanish, Math, and public speaking/debate. We are committed to helping students overcome challenges in these areas.
How do I get help with public speaking?
You can sign up for help through our "Get Help" page. Our tutors will work with you one-on-one to improve your speaking and debating skills.
Is there a cost for the services?
All services are free for students. Speak Up Scholars is a non-profit organization committed to helping students.
